Questions

Frequently asked.

Do you offer same-day laundry?

Yes. Drop-offs before 10:00 AM Mon–Sat are eligible for same-day return, subject to load size.

What areas do you serve?

Midrand, Ivory Park, Tembisa, Kyalami, Halfway House and surrounding Johannesburg areas.

Is pickup & delivery free?

Free pickup & delivery is included on the Express plan and on commercial schedules.

How do I pay?

Cash, EFT, or card on collection. Commercial accounts are billed monthly.

Do you handle delicates and dry cleaning?

Absolutely — suits, dresses, curtains and bedding are all handled with specialist care.
